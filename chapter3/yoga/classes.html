Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asna (proper alignment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to smaple this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 60 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
