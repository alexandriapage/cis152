The Family Behind the Nature Getaway

Our story began with a deep love for Big Sur and the beauty of nature. The Page family was inspired by the breathtaking landscapes and peaceful surroundings, and they dreamed of making it easier for others to experience it too. With that vision, they created a welcoming resort where guests can relax, explore, and connect with nature in comfort. Today, we continue that mission by offering unforgettable stays that bring people closer to the natural world.

As a family-founded resort, our mission is to create a welcoming space where guests feel at home while connecting with the beauty of nature. We are committed to providing comfortable stays, meaningful experiences, and genuine hospitality, so every visit becomes a lasting memory.
