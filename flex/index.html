Located in the Heart of Sedona

At Casita Sedona, you'll be close to art galleries, shops, restaurants, hiking trails, and tours. Ride the free trolley to explore Sedona with ease.

Luxurious Rooms

Stay in well-appointed rooms with fireplaces, king-size beds, balconies with red rock views, and private baths with whirlpool tubs. Choose from Canyon & Javelina!

Hearty Breakfast

Each day begins with Belgian waffles, pumpkin French toast, or quiche, plus fresh fruit, yogurt, juice, and coffee.

About Your Hosts

Melanie and Greg Tortuga are long-time Sedona residents who love sharing their knowledge. Melanie is an author and artist; Greg has years of experience guiding jeep tours and hikes.

Guided Hikes

Casita Sedona offers a free guided hike each week. Explore the Seven Sacred Pools along the Soldiers Pass Trail with our knowledgeable hosts.
